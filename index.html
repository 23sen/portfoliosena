The approach

Nothing moved on a hunch. I ran continuous A/B tests on audiences and creative, read the numbers every week, and shifted budget toward whatever converted best. That testing discipline is what compounded into the 3.42× ROAS and 289% growth, and it is the same method I bring to any account.
